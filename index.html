Client Stories The Proof

Both client stories are now populated with real content. Boeing leads with the provisioning transformation (5 business days to 15 minutes) and the Flashbuild as proof of Claude-powered delivery at enterprise scale. The "8x faster delivery" stat comes from the estimated 2+ months without AI tooling vs. under one week with Claude. Natera covers the health and life sciences vertical with the FDA-regulated environment, organic bottom-up adoption, and a clear path from one team to 80+.

Boeing "Big Ideas" for deeper conversations: Three plays for the microsite or follow-up: an AI-powered developer onboarding agent (current onboarding is "severely broken" with weeks of serialized access requests), an FFU/TMR compliance evidence generator (cutting 47-day ATO prep to hours), and an SRE reasoning agent for platform stabilization. Natera "Big Ideas" for deeper conversations: Five plays: AI-accelerated FDA/MasterControl compliance, enterprise release intelligence across 80+ teams, a clinical genomics co-pilot for variant classification, company-wide AI fluency as a platform, and agentic SDLC quality orchestration across their CI/CD stack. These are the "if Anthropic invested directly" stories for the Health & Life Sciences GTM lead.
